I am currently a senior year computer science undergrad at [Delhi Technological University](www.dtu.ac.in) (formerly Delhi College of Engineering), New Delhi, India. This fall, I will be starting as Ph.D. student in computer science at [Columbia University](www.columbia.edu), advised by [Prof. Brian A. Smith](http://www.cs.columbia.edu/~brian/index.html).

## Research Interests: AI for people!

My primary research interest revolves around answering the question, ***"How can machines replicate human perception capabilities?"***. To this end - I design, develop, and deploy technology that enables people to have an immersive experience while interacting with machines. Beyond this research focus, my motivation is to put AI, and its applications, in the hands of people who have been left out of the technological revolution. Hence, my research is directed towards building interactive systems for people with disabilities to better experience the world around them!
