I am currently a senior year computer science undergrad at [Delhi Technological University](www.dtu.ac.in) (formerly Delhi College of Engineering), New Delhi, India. This fall, I will be starting as Ph.D. student in computer science at [Columbia University](www.columbia.edu), advised by [Prof. Brian A. Smith](http://www.cs.columbia.edu/~brian/index.html).

## Research Interests

My primary research interest revolves around answering the question, ***"How can machines replicate human perception capabilities?"***. Hence, my objective is to build artificially intelligent systems that comprehend the visual world around them, as humans do.

Over the past decade, artificial intelligence has made significant strides both theoretically and in its applications. Yet, the applications of AI are hindered by the lack of parallel advancement in computer vision.



I plan to dedicate my academic pursuits and career, whether in academia or in the research industry, to
develop algorithms for machines to match human perception capabilities. Beyond this research focus, my motivation is to put AI, and its applications, in the hands of people who have been left out of the technological revolution.
